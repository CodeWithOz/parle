# TEF Canada – Expression Orale (Oral Expression)

## Test Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Test Name** | Oral expression (« Expression orale ») |
| **Compulsory** | Yes |
| **Duration** | 15 minutes |
| **Scoring Range** | 0 to 699 |

## Test Structure

### Sections

The test consists of two sections:

- **Section A**: Getting information
- **Section B**: Arguing to convince

## Test Objective

Express yourself orally in French in an organized and detailed way. Discuss various topics and support your arguments.

## Expected Skills

During the oral expression test, you will be asked to:

- Ask well-structured questions appropriate to a specific context
- Argue precisely and clearly
- Know how to structure an oral presentation
- Speak fluently and spontaneously while being convincing and keeping a clear structure in your speech

## Evaluation Criteria

### Assessment Process

Two examiners will assess your oral expression skills:
1. The first examiner will be with you during the test and will record it
2. The recording will be sent to a second examiner for a second opinion

### Evaluation Focus

The examiners will pay close attention to:

- **Language Level**: Grammar and syntax, vocabulary variety and accuracy, pronunciation, fluency
- **Speech Structure**: Your ease in interacting and ability to structure your speech (cohesion, clear development)
- **Adaptability**: Your ability to adapt to different communication situations
